Our Team

Meet the dedicated researchers and experts driving our community resilience studies.

Project Leads

Dr. Cynthia Chen

Principal Investigator

Professor in the Civil & Environmental Engineering Department and the Industrial & Systems Engineering Department at the University of Washington, Seattle


Contribution: Dr. Chen is responsible for leading the entire research team, overseeing all 4 thrusts and community engagement, and making scientific, administrative, and budget-related decisions (with the consultation of the team) to ensure that the project meets all expected goals. Led the communication with the Laurelhurst community and participated in workshops and guided MVP development.

Dr. Dan Abramson

Co-Principal Investigator

Associate Professor in the Department of Urban Design and Planning at the University of Washington, Seattle


Contribution: Dr. Abramson supported project management and planning with a focus on urban planning and social science and led the communication with the Westport community. As co-lead for Broadening Participation in the Cascadia CoPes Hazards Research Hub, Dr. Abramson will facilitate Ocosta School student roles in the collaboration with Westport/South Beach.

Dr. Kurtis Heimerl

Co-Principal Investigator

Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle


Contribution: Supported project management and planning with a focus on technical elements. Advised CSE students. Dr. Heimerl's team have been using the SCN network to run and support several educational initiatives, and will bring this educational outreach material to new partner communities of Laurelhurst and Westport.

Dr. Tyler McCormick

Co-Principal Investigator

Associate Professor of Statistics and Sociology at the University of Washington, Seattle


Contribution: Supported project management and planning with a focus on social science. Dr. McCormick is working closely with the team on advising the team on the modified-ARD survey, model development for information dissemination, and designing strategies for information dissemination.

Dr. Shuai Huang

Co-Principal Investigator

Associate Professor in the Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle


Contribution: Supported project management and planning.
